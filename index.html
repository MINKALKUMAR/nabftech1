About the Summit

India’s Non-Banking Financial Companies (NBFCs) are vital in providing credit to underserved sectors, from rural businesses to MSMEs. As technology reshapes finance, NBFCs and fintech are collaborating to create innovative, inclusive solutions.

We are excited to announce the NBFC & Fintech Synergy Summit 2025, set for August. This premier event will be a hub for networking, knowledge-sharing, and collaboration among NBFC leaders, fintech innovators, policymakers, investors, and tech providers.

The summit, with the theme “Reimagining NBFCs: Catalysts for a New Financial Era” will feature keynotes, panel discussions, and interactive sessions on emerging trends, regulatory insights, and digital transformation. Join us in shaping the future of finance!

Target Audience

The NBFC Summit is designed for professionals and stakeholders across the financial services industry, including:

  • CEOs, CFOs, CTOs, and CXOs of NBFCs
  • Senior Management from Banks and Financial Institutions
  • Regulators and Policy Makers
  • FinTech Innovators and Startups
  • Investment Bankers and Venture Capitalists
  • Consultants and Advisors in the Financial Sector
  • Academicians and Researchers

If you are involved in shaping the future of finance, this summit is for you.

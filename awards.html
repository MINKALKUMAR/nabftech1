Nomination Guidelines

All nominations will be treated as strictly confidential.
Applicants must submit the official online nomination form.
Multiple categories can be selected while filling out the nomination form.
A nomination fee is applicable at this stage to ensure an unbiased process.
Discounts: 5–10 Categories = 10%, More than 10 Categories = 15%.
Applications will be reviewed by an external jury panel.
The jury panel will determine the winners for each category.
The nomination fee is non-refundable under any circumstances.
Winners will be notified 15 days before the ceremony.
